Cheese cake

A classic cheese cake

Cheese cake is always a hit and the recipe might be the best cheese cake recipe you'll ever try

Ingredients

Crust

  • graham crackers
  • butter
  • sugar
  • salt

    • filling

  • cream cheese
  • sugar
  • sour cream
  • vanilla extract
  • eggs

Steps

  1. mix graham crakers with melted butter granulated sugar and a pinch of salt
  2. press into the bottom and slightly up the sides of your spring form pan
  3. combine cream cheese,sugar,vanilla extract in a bowl and mix
  4. Add sour cream,eggs and mix
  5. add salt and flour and mix
  6. pour filling on pan with crust and bake until it is slightly jiggly
